(Bad) People

Darth Vader

This is Darth Vader. He likes red light sabers, asphyxiation and the Dark Side.

Jabba The Hutt

Jabba lives in his mansion on Tatooine, with his servants and pet rancor. He has a derogatory attitude to princesses, and a fondness for chains and bondage.

Boba Fett

Boba Fett is a bounty hunter, mostly known for delivering Han Solo to Jabba The Hutt. Last seen falling into Sarlacc's mouth.
